Because this is The Historical Herald and I want you to get the best information, I'd like to mention that all of the events really did happen to the best of our knowledge. However, the ullamalitzi game (the sports article) sits in that blurry line between fact and legend. It's likely that the two leaders did play a game, butthe written records appear only afrter the Spanish conquered Tenochtitlan. For this reason, many historians believe the story is embellished to fit a narrative.

Also, while I did my best to try to find out the proper values of the currencies (and I think they're pretty accurate), it's highly likely they're not exact. Saying a pennyweight is 1.55g of gold is a modern addition there to help readers understand better. To put the values into perspective, that same amont of (pure) gold is roughly 200 USD today.
